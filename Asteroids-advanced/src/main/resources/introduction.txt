Operation Introduction:

Up Arrow: Thrust Forward

Left Arrow: Rotate Left

Right Arrow: Rotate Right

Spacebar: Fire Bullet

A Button: Hyperspace Jump

Player is invincible at the beginning of the game, after a hyperspace jump to another location, and after dying.

Player is killed if collides with asteroid or alien ship, or is it with an alien ship bullet.

Player score is added to high score list after game ends.
